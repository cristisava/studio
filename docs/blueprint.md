# **App Name**: ATFR Website

## Core Features:

- Homepage Hero: Display academy name, mission, background image, and CTAs on the home page.
- Organization Information: Present information about the organization's mission, values, and history on the 'About Us' page.
- Program Overview: Showcase training programs, youth development initiatives, and workshops on the 'Programs' page.
- Photo Gallery: Display photos from training sessions, events, and matches in an image gallery.
- Team Member Profiles: Display team coaches, mentors, and management with photos and roles.
- Partner Logos: Show logos of the organization's partners and sponsors.
- Contact Form: Implement a contact form, display email, phone, and a map embed.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Forest green (#386641) inspired by the field, symbolizing growth and potential.
- Background color: Off-white (#FAFAFA) provides a clean and modern look.
- Accent color: Dark gray (#525B54) to convey professionalism.
- Body and headline font: 'PT Sans', a humanist sans-serif, will provide a balance of modernity and approachability for both headlines and body text.
- Use simple, outline-style icons related to football and the organization's activities.
- Employ a clean, modern layout with plenty of whitespace to ensure ease of navigation and readability.
- Subtle animations for page transitions and interactions to enhance the user experience without being distracting.
